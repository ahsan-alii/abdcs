Services

ROADS

Several projects of dual carriage way system have been successfully executed by ACS. We have the requisite machinery suitable for variety of areas according to the topography ranging from plain land to rocky terrain for carrying out earthwork, thanks to our large fleet of earth moving machinery which enables the production of a high-quality work. Our equipment and expertise also enables us to undertake such assignments in areas where the traffic density is high and the job requires speedy completion of the project with negligible inconvenience to public, within the assigned time frame.

SEWERAGE SYSTEM

We specialize in planning and construction of sewerage system to cater for growing needs of modern living and communities.

WATER SUPPLY SYSTEM

Water is the most essential and basic need of any community. Water management in metropolitans is need of the hour due to depleting natural reservoirs. We have specialized in efficient water supply systems without wasting this precious resource.

STORM WATER DRAINAGE SYSTEM

In any modern community living a major hazard to the infrastructure is storm water. Its proper disposal is of paramount importance for a comfortable, hygienic and safe living. Proper defining of catchment areas, designing and construction of storm water drainage system is one of our specialties. We have successfully executed and completed numerous projects for laying storm water drainage system throughout the country specially in hilly terrain.

LANDSCAPING

The Company has necessary re-enforcement to conduct large scale survey of land and carryout landscaping works. We have done development works of various societies, in this regard several successful projects have been undertaken by ACS.

BUILDINGS

With all the expertise in the utilities and services, the company today has all the ingredients to engineer and construct high rise buildings. Our reputation is a testament for both modern and contemporary structures completed on fast track bases with the allocated time and budget.

About Us

Abdullah Construction Services (ACS) is a registered Civil Engineering Company of national repute with its headquarters at Islamabad. ACS was established with a vision to conceive innovative ideas in the construction industry and making them a reality. It carries an infrastructure for Planning, Designing and Construction of Civil Engineering Projects, Roads, Highways, Landscaping, Storm Water, Drainage Systems, Sewerage and Water Supply Systems & Buildings.
